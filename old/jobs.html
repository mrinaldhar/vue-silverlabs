Join The Vue Team!

Location: Bay area or Hyderabad only

Shopping, in the real world, is everything from hanging out with BFFs, staring at clothes, trying them on, exchanging comments on looks & (some) buying! Vue is about bringing that holistic feeling to life! Vue is a mobile app bringing the joys of styling with friends in a uniquely personal way.

The team is composed of an accomplished group of people from Google, startups, Qualcomm and PARC. Collectively, this is a team that has brought features used by hundreds of millions of users every day, launched sticky apps that have had millions of loyal users, authored 150+ issued/pending patents, and had an amazing track record of building highly productive teams.

On the technology front, we deal with large amounts of crawled products, metadata extraction (text + image), large numbers of user generated content (mostly images), social metadata (connected graphs, mentions, conversations), image analysis for style, determining relevance based on a multi-dimensional influencer analysis (location, semantic places, brands, behaviors, etc.) and much more. We bring an intersection of data mining, behavioral science, curation and context together towards a simple and rewarding user experience at a sentimental level.

Open Positions

Crawling & Curation - Intern/Part Time

Be part of the early content team, bringing to life fun content and metadata on our platform. If you can think in Python, have used scrapy and enjoys intelligent extraction of metadata from crawled products, you are in for a lot of fun!

iOS Engineer - Intern/Full Time

If you like building amazing user experiences and are an Objective-C aficionado, you are going to want to explore what we are building! It’s social, it’s showing off your style, it’s chatting with friends around interesting looks, and a lot more! Be part of the early team and make an impact.

Backend Engineer - Full Time

Our backend engineer would ideally know Google App Engine, but more generally, be strong in Java and NoSql systems (having built a Cassandra based system, for e.g., would be very useful). If you are excited about scalable infrastructure and in bringing responsive backends to life in complex social network settings, you won’t be disappointed!

Data Mining & Analysis - Intern

Does spending your summer doing social behavior and user engagement analysis sound exciting to you? Do you like the challenge of extracting style from text and image metadata? We want to talk to you! Having previously worked with hybrid text+image classifiers, social ranking algorithms, location clustering, etc. is of high value.

You will be working with one of the best teams in the startup scene! Interested candidates may reach out to vidya@thesilverlabs.com.
